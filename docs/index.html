Data Source

NOAA High-Resolution Rapid Refresh (HRRR) model, ~3 km grid spacing, updated hourly. Data served from NOAA AWS S3 bucket via the Herbie Python package.

Probability Index

Composite of five ingredients weighted by predictive skill: 0–3 km UH (38%), Computed STP — CAPE×SRH₁×BS₀₋₆ (28%), 0–3 km SRH (18%), Surface CAPE (10%), 0–6 km bulk shear (6%). UH gating suppresses false-positive areas lacking mesocyclone rotation.

25-Mile Radius

Raw gridded probabilities are convolved with a Gaussian kernel scaled to a ~25 statute mile radius to reflect positional uncertainty and produce smooth, SPC-style probability fields.

Limitations

Experimental product — not for operational use. HRRR-based tornado probabilities carry large uncertainty, particularly beyond 3 h. Always consult official NWS/SPC guidance for severe weather decisions.
